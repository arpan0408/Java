A paragraph is a self-contained unit of discourse in writing that deals with a particular point or idea. It typically consists of several sentences that are unified and coherent, meaning they work together to express a single thought clearly. The length of a paragraph can vary, but it is generally defined by its content rather than a specific number of sentences. The purpose of a paragraph is to organize ideas and provide clarity in writing, ensuring that each paragraph focuses on a distinct topic or argument.
